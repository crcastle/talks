The Enterprise Service Bus Is Dead! Long Live the Enterprise Service Bus

The enterprise service bus (ESB) was designed to contain the complexity of connecting heterogeneous applications, including the entire organization’s business logic and data. These concepts have fallen out of favor with the advent of microservices and Kafka. In this talk, we’ll introduce a modern definition of the ESB, with Kafka at its heart.
